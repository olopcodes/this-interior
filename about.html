About us

Started by a first generation immigrant, Alex fell in love with designs. In order to pursue this passion, Alex went on to study and the prestigious Peoples School of Design, graduating with honors. After college, Alex joined the design firm, Designers Inc. NYC. It is here where knowledge and a keen eye of visual hierarchy was honed

After a few year the work at the firm became repetitive and it was time for a change. Taking a risk, This Interiors was started in 2020. Not the best of times because of the pandemic; however, the number of people moving out of their residences and advances in technology, aided in the flourishing of the business.

Since then This Interiors has garnered much praise from clients and critics alike. We look forward to continue our success and making dreams reality.
